now that us is just a word 
no longer with you as one 
your name is but a detail 
of the person I’m with 
our bodies a hindrance 
to us further interweaving 
as a garden of Eden 
a timeless paradise 
an indulgence for life 
prior to our melding 
past suffering and battling 
futile in reexamination.

mosquito zooming,
fridge buzzing,
skies growling
and a faint ticking of the clock.
I hear you breathing
and our feet touch,
ever so slightly.

              ⁓
On and on, and on
I move.
one place to the other.
I never stand still,
I’d rather not smother.
when slowing down wasn’t my will
love has died,
friends and family pushed away.
When standing in my stride
time’s a hunter and i am It’s prey.
Nowhere I can call home,
nothing for me outside.
Eternal traveller syndrome,
with no place but thoughts to hide.
Push me away,
but keep me close
for i am just a stray
with a tendency to self impose.
                 ⁓

              ⁓ 
Mistress, I’ll obey.
I bend to your way.
To the whip with me,
lash me some lasting scars.
Turn me to ash, 
burn me to cinder.
How you’ll be able to do
it so tender.
Like a moth being slowly 
drawn to the flame.
There is no shame, 
in taking reign 
over my hollowed mind.
Yet, you do it so blind.
Do it all with a grind, 
reduce me to sin.
You might have spent my fuel, 
but I’ll carry on being your mule
for if this love is exertion, 
I’ll be your employee until desertion.

                     ⁓
Perhaps It’s the way of the modern day-when we think about 
which hashtag to use next
while people are dying,
parents crying, politics lying,
for profit or for power.
For personal belief,
because it is easy 
to warp the minds of those
who fear or grief.
Online is the place to debate,
their biggest achievement to deprecate.
Don’t people ever get tired
of all the hate or the need to post an update?
